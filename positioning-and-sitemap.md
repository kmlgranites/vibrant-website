# Vibrant — Positioning & Sitemap

## 1. Who Vibrant is (from your content)

Vibrant Future Tech Services ("Vibrant") has, since 2014, been a behind-the-scenes
production partner to publishing houses worldwide. You take a manuscript, a rough
Manga, a stack of scanned pages — and hand back something print-ready, error-free,
and beautiful. Your range is unusually wide:

- **Content** — development, supplements, alt-text, enhancement
- **Editorial** — copyediting, proofreading, indexing
- **Production** — typesetting (InDesign, 3B2, QuarkXPress, LaTeX, FrameMaker)
- **Content Transformation** — Word/PDF/XML/HTML + eBook (EPUB/MOBI)
- **Creative** — artwork, graphics processing, image editing & restoration
- **Manga Studio** — retouching, lettering, scanning *(your emotional core)*
- **Business Process Management** — research, data, forms

## 2. The positioning problem

The current site lists *everything you can do* — but a buyer can't feel *why you*.
A publisher choosing a production partner is buying **trust, quality, and a deadline
met**. That's the story the site has to tell, not a service menu.

## 3. The positioning we'll run with

> **"Second to none."** *(nulli secundus — your own vision, made the promise.)*
>
> **Vibrant is the studio behind the page.** We're the team publishers trust to
> turn manuscripts and raw Manga into polished, print-ready, reader-ready work —
> editorial, production, creative, and a dedicated Manga Studio, all under one roof.

Three things this does:
1. **Leads with trust + craft**, not a service list.
2. **Makes Manga the hero** — it's where you're differentiated and emotional, and
   where your testimonials live.
3. Keeps the **full breadth** one click away for the publisher who needs data,
   typesetting, or conversion at scale.

### Differentiators to repeat across the site
- One partner, manuscript → finished book (no juggling 5 vendors)
- A real **Manga Studio** with letterers, retouchers, scanning
- **"Services to suit every budget"** — your own line; great for indies + big houses
- Values that show up in the work: People · Agility · Teamwork · Integrity · Excellence
- *"We ask the right questions, we listen, and we deliver brilliance."*

## 4. Sitemap

| Page | Job it does |
|---|---|

| Page | Why it's worth adding |

`About · Services · Manga Studio · Work · Contact` + a button **"Get a quote"**

> Services is a dropdown/mega-menu exposing the 7 families so the breadth is
